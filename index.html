What is ShadowPing

The Invisible Eye on Your Discord Servers

ShadowPing operates silently in the background — a ghost on your Discord servers — watching every new member who walks through the door. The moment someone joins, you know. No delays, no missed opportunities, no manual checking.

Built for community managers, marketers, and growth operators who understand that timing is everything. When a new member joins your target server, the window to reach them is small. ShadowPing gives you that window — and keeps it open.

From basic real-time alerts to fully automated Pro-tier friend request outreach, ShadowPing is your silent intelligence layer on Discord.

Deep Dive

Built Different. Designed for Results.

ShadowPing was built from the ground up with one goal in mind: give operators complete awareness of who is joining Discord communities, and the tools to act on that intelligence instantly. In a space where most tools are clunky bots or complex scripts, ShadowPing brings a clean, mobile-first experience that anyone can use.

At its core, ShadowPing works by connecting a dedicated Discord account — a "monitor account" — to your chosen servers. This account silently listens for member join events across every server it belongs to. The moment someone joins, the event is captured, processed by our backend, and delivered to your mobile app in real time via WebSocket. No polling, no lag. Just instant awareness.

For Basic plan users, this means a live feed of every new member across all your monitored servers. You can see who joined, which server they joined, their account age, and when it happened. Each event is logged, browsable, and copyable — so you can take your outreach wherever you need to.

Pro plan users unlock the full automation stack. Connect dedicated friend request accounts, assign each one to a specific target server, and let ShadowPing handle the rest. When a new member joins your target server, the assigned FR account automatically sends them a friend request — with configurable delays, daily limits, and cooldowns to keep everything safe and natural. You can run multiple accounts simultaneously, each targeting a different server, all managed from one clean interface.

Security and control are built in. Monitor accounts never send friend requests — they only watch. Friend request accounts are separate, dedicated, and fully controlled by you. Username filters let Pro users exclude certain accounts from outreach. The admin panel gives full control over pricing, user management, subscription codes, and support links — all from within the app.

Payments are handled through NowPayments, supporting Bitcoin, USDT, Ethereum, Litecoin, and dozens of other cryptocurrencies. Subscriptions are activated instantly on payment, with deep link integration returning users directly to the app. Referral codes reward your community — every referred user that subscribes earns you 3 bonus days automatically.
